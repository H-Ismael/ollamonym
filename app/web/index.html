Where Teams Get Blocked Today

Sensitive Text Is Often the Last Barrier to Cloud-Hosted and/or Proprietary LLM Adoption

Support and CRM Summaries

Tickets include names, phones, emails, addresses, and account references. Without a privacy layer, legal and security often block external model usage.

Contract and Legal Review

Drafts contain client identities, obligations, and commercial terms. Teams want clause extraction and QA, but raw uploads are restricted.

Healthcare and Insurance Ops

Case notes and claims text can contain direct identifiers. Operational AI is delayed unless data is transformed before model calls.

Internal Copilots for Analysts

Analysts need LLM help on sensitive emails, incidents, and audit notes. Pseudonymization enables utility while reducing direct exposure risk.

Finance, KYC, and Fraud Ops

Transaction narratives and investigation notes contain account references, identity fragments, and high-risk metadata that should not be sent raw to external models.

HR and Internal Investigations

Case files include employee identifiers, allegations, and private correspondence. Teams need analysis support without disclosing direct identities.
